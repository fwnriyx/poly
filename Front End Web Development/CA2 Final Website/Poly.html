Integrating into Poly life

I entered Singapore Polytechnic as a Year 1 in April 2022. My experience so far has been nothing less than delightful. Despite being from different backgrounds, the activities held on the orientation day helped us to know each other better as classmates, which helped to spark more conversations and friendships. It is much more different than secondary school as it is more self oriented and I had to work on my time management. In my opinion, DCITP is a good course as the workload is not too high and it sets the stepping stones for us before we progress into the next semester At the end of this semester, I am required to choose between Infocomm Security Management(DISM), Information Technology(DIT), or Applied AI and Analytics(DAAA)! DISM

In SP, Diploma in Infocomm Security Management (DISM) students will be learning more about security technology, security management, information technology, law and teamwork and communications. They will also have an internship in year 3 that lasts for roughly 22 weeks. Some of the career options that are available include: Cyber Risk Analyst, Forensic Investigator and Incident Investigator. In my opinion, this is my most preferred course as I have the most interest in the career options as well as the topics taught throughout the next few years. Learn more here.

DAAA

In SP, Diploma in Applied AI and Analytics (DAAA) students are able to learn how to develop AI applications that benefits our world. They constantly receive new challenges that they get to pick at. Some of the topics they will learn are Data Engineering, Deep Learning as well as Data Structures & Algorithm. In my opinion, I am slightly interested in this course as it broadens my knowledge regarding the technology that we use around us, but I am not too interested in going in depth into these topics. Hence, I am more interested in this course than DIT, but less than DISM. Learn more here.
